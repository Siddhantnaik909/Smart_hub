How it works

This calculator estimates runtime based on capacity and load.

  • Capacity: The total energy storage in milliamp-hours (mAh).
  • Consumption: The current draw of your device in milliamps (mA).
  • Efficiency: A 70% efficiency factor is applied to account for real-world conditions (heat, age, conversion loss).
